Privacy Policy — Higher Stairs

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how the Higher Stairs game ("App", "we", "our") handles and protects the privacy and personal information of its users.

1. General Information

Higher Stairs is a casual arcade game in which the player launches a geometric shape up an endless staircase. The gameplay itself does not require an account, sign-in, or any personal information.

2. Data We Collect

The App itself does not ask you for, or directly collect, personal data such as your name, email, contacts, photos, or precise location. Your score and best result are kept only on your device.

However, the App displays advertisements through Google AdMob (see Section 4). To serve ads, AdMob and Google may automatically collect certain information, including: a device advertising identifier (such as the Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA), IP address, device and operating-system information, and basic app-interaction data.

3. How We Use Data

Information processed by the App and its advertising partner is used only to: run and display the game, keep your local score, and show and measure advertisements (including showing personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region and choices).

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show banner and full-screen (interstitial) ads. AdMob is provided by Google and may use device identifiers and usage data to deliver and measure ads. We do not control the data collected by Google.

You can learn how Google uses this data here: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from AdMob. On most devices you can limit ad personalization or reset your advertising identifier in the system settings.

5. Data Storage and Transmission

The App does not run its own servers and does not store your personal data in the cloud. Game progress (such as your best score) stays on your device. Advertising data is transmitted to and handled by Google in accordance with Google's policies.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

• Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads — https://policies.google.com/privacy

• Google Play Services (on Android) — https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Permissions

The App requests only the permissions required to run and to display ads (for example, internet access). It does not request access to your contacts, files, camera, microphone, or precise location.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Security

We take reasonable, industry-standard measures to protect user information. Please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

10. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Policy as needed. Any changes will be published on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
