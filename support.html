Common questions

The leaderboard doesn't show my score.

Make sure you are signed in to Apple Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android). The leaderboard tab opens the native sign-in sheet if you are signed out.

My best score reset.

The local best resets when the app is reinstalled. Your global leaderboard entry under Game Center / Play Games is kept by the platform and will return once you sign in.

Ads are showing too often.

A full-screen ad runs roughly every two or three restarts. If an ad ever feels broken (won't close), please email us with your device model and the OS version.

How do I update the app?

Updates are delivered through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). If the app shows a "Please update" screen on launch, tapping the button will take you to the store page.
